What are Reminders Through Association?

You create an association in the real world that will remind you to do someting.

Focus on an object that is very strange, or related, or will be at the location when you want to do the task. For example, a funny pen you have on your desk, which will be there when you need to work.

Focus on this item, and then associate it with a task you need to do. For example, tell yourself "When I see my funny pen, I will write that email to Bob."


Studies have shown that reminders through association is one of the most effective methods of reminding yourself to do something.

Traditional electronic reminders often get flooded out or ignored.
